Test your knowledge!

Fun at parties or trivia nights, Celebrity: Dead or Alive is an app that lets you determine just how much of a trivia genius you are. Select "Alive" or "Dead" for each celebrity and gain a point when correct. See if you can reach the max of 10 points!

Play solo!

Celebrity: Dead or Alive can be used to pass time on the train or waiting for class to start. If you have a competitive streak, try to beat the fastest player on the leaderboards.

Challenge your friends!

Show off your score or beat your friends' scores. Prove you are the trivia master amongst your friends.
